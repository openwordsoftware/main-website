Vision

This project was born out of a vision and desire to see Christian believers all over the world have access to search the sermons of William Branham in their own language. Very good tools and translations exist for English speakers but are more limited for those that speak other languages. The Message Hub platform was developed to support translation efforts worldwide, and it has been a great success. The next natural progression is developing advanced tools that take advantage of this platform and all the hard work that has gone into it and the translations. There are in existence mobile apps that utilize the Message Hub that support iOS and Android devices. While these app are very convenient and useful, those apps and mobile devices in general are by design more limited than full PC desktop and laptop computers. This project is aimed at fulfilling that need.

Openwordsoftware.com was created to support development of these type of software tools with the guiding principle that the sermons of William Branham should be readily available and searchable by anyone in the world, regardless of their language. We are not interested in profit or credit, but in accomplishing this mission.
